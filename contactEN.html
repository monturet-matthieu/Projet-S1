About us

Who am I?

My name is Matthieu Monturet, I am a first-year student of MMI (Multimedia and Internet Business) in Montbéliard.

Context of the creation of the site

This website was created in response to a request for a biannual project on the theme of feminism. I chose Emma Watson as a figure of feminism because she is a person known to many people, both for her acting career and for her actions to raise awareness of the feminist movements she supports. As a result, it will attract more people because it will inspire a sense of knowledge and curiosity about it. On the other hand, this project makes it possible to address a serious subject while at the same time raising awareness among an audience that is not necessarily accustomed to this kind of discourse. The defence of women’s rights and the pursuit of gender equality is a topical issue, but it is nonetheless important
